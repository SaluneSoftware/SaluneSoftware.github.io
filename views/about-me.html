Hello! I am a Boston-based creator and musician aiming to make something new and interesting. Whether it's game audio and interactivity or making conceptual albums, my aim is to get as close as I can to becoming a magician with music.

My approach is to learn as much as I can about techniques and systems so that I can scramble everything, hopefully correctly, into something I like. I love discovering interesting ways to tie music to emotion.

I am currently a 6th semester at The Berklee College of Music, majoring in Electronic Production and Sound Design as well as Game and Interactive Media, and with a specialization in Sound Design for Games.
