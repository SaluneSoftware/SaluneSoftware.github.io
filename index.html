The Band

Meet Cornelius

Cornelius Eclipse AKA Frosted Flake is an veteran DJ and turn-tablist from Wapakoneta, Ohio. He got his nickname after a legendary DJ set at the Frosted Flakes 10 Year Anniversary Party in Anaheim, CA that went semi-viral on Vine. Some say he has since fell off, but his crucial part in DimFace’s success tells a different story. His hypersonic beats will itch your brain like never before this summer.

Meet DARK GOAT

Oier Aguirre, also known as The Dark Goat, started his music path when he was just 7 years old. He started as a classical percussionist and singer, but later on decided to move to the dark side of music (Bach would have not like it) after realizing that subwoofers were not part of the usual setup in an orchestra. His first approach to music production and DJ-ing was in Minecraft, when he discovered that by putting note blocks together he could do amazing covers such as “Electric Twinkle Twinkle”, his first big hit. After that, he decided to switch to Ableton and start his big journey by joining the “DIMFACE The Texno Trio”.
