// 02 — About

About
Me

Hello! I am a Boston-based creator and musician aiming to make new and interesting audio. Whether it's game audio/interactivity or making conceptual albums, my aim is to get as close as I can to becoming a magician with music.

My approach is to learn as much as I can about techniques and systems so that I can scramble and break everything into something I like. I love discovering interesting ways to tie music to emotion.

I am currently a 6th semester at The Berklee College of Music, majoring in Electronic Production and Sound Design as well as Game and interactive media, with a specialization in Sound Design for Games.
